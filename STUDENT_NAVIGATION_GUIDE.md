## ğŸ“ How to Answer a Question

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Find a Question**
   - Navigate using any of the methods above
   - Look for questions that interest you or that you can help with

2. **Click on the Question**
   - Click the question title to open the question detail page
   - You'll see:
     - Full question content
     - Question author
     - Number of views and votes
     - Existing answers (if any)

3. **Write Your Answer**
   - Scroll down to the answer form
   - Enter your answer in the text area
   - Optionally attach an image (JPG/PNG, max 5MB)
   - Click "Post Answer"

4. **Answer Posted!**
   - Your answer will appear immediately
   - The question author will be notified
   - You'll earn karma points when your answer is upvoted
